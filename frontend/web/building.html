Salesforce Tower San Francisco

The complete Building Information Model (BIM) for Salesforce Tower, the tallest building in San Francisco and a marvel of sustainable design.

Building Overview

  • Height: 1,070 feet (326 meters)
  • Floors: 61 stories above ground, 4 below
  • Total Area: 1.4 million square feet
  • Completed: 2018
  • Certification: LEED Platinum

Sustainability Features

The building incorporates numerous sustainable design features:

  • High-performance curtain wall with triple-glazed windows
  • On-site blackwater treatment plant
  • 100% renewable energy usage
  • Advanced building automation systems
  • Green roof with native plantings
