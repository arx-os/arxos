## Introduction

Welcome to the Arxos community! These guidelines establish the standards for participation in our ecosystem, ensuring a safe, productive, and inclusive environment for all users. Our platform serves construction professionals, developers, and stakeholders working to advance building technology.

### Purpose

These guidelines aim to:
- Foster a collaborative and respectful community
- Maintain professional standards in construction technology
- Protect users from harassment and abuse
- Ensure quality contributions and discussions
- Provide clear escalation paths for violations

### Scope

These guidelines apply to all Arxos platform interactions including:
- Draft submissions and proposals
- Comments and discussions
- Collaboration sessions
- Funding contributions
- Documentation and resources
- Private messages and communications

---

## Core Principles

### 1. Professional Excellence
We maintain high standards for construction technology and professional conduct. All contributions should reflect expertise, accuracy, and industry best practices.

### 2. Respect and Inclusion
Every community member deserves respect regardless of background, experience level, or role. Harassment, discrimination, or exclusionary behavior will not be tolerated.

### 3. Constructive Collaboration
We encourage productive discussions that advance building technology. Disagreements should be handled respectfully with focus on solutions rather than personal attacks.

### 4. Safety and Compliance
All content must comply with relevant building codes, safety standards, and legal requirements. We prioritize safety in construction technology discussions.

### 5. Transparency and Accountability
Moderation decisions are transparent and appealable. Users are accountable for their contributions and can expect fair treatment.

## Community Standards

### Professional Conduct

#### ✅ Expected Behavior
- **Expertise Sharing**: Share knowledge and experience constructively
- **Respectful Dialogue**: Engage in civil, professional discussions
- **Quality Contributions**: Provide well-researched, accurate information
- **Collaborative Spirit**: Work together to solve construction challenges
- **Safety Focus**: Prioritize safety in all technical discussions
- **Constructive Feedback**: Offer helpful, specific feedback
- **Mentorship**: Help newcomers learn and grow
- **Innovation**: Contribute to advancing construction technology

#### ❌ Unacceptable Behavior
- **Harassment**: Bullying, intimidation, or targeted attacks
- **Discrimination**: Bias based on race, gender, experience, or background
- **Spam**: Irrelevant, repetitive, or promotional content
- **Misinformation**: Deliberately false or misleading information
- **Safety Violations**: Promoting unsafe practices or non-compliance
- **Plagiarism**: Claiming others' work as your own
- **Trolling**: Deliberately disruptive or inflammatory behavior
- **Impersonation**: Pretending to be someone else

### Content Quality Standards

#### Draft Submissions
- **Accuracy**: All technical information must be correct and verifiable
- **Completeness**: Provide sufficient detail for implementation
- **Safety**: Ensure compliance with relevant codes and standards
- **Originality**: Submit original work or properly attribute sources
- **Clarity**: Use clear, professional language and formatting

#### Comments and Discussions
- **Relevance**: Stay on topic and contribute meaningfully
- **Helpfulness**: Provide constructive, actionable feedback
- **Respect**: Disagree professionally without personal attacks
- **Accuracy**: Correct misinformation when possible
- **Context**: Consider the full discussion before responding

#### Collaboration Sessions
- **Participation**: Engage actively and constructively
- **Respect**: Value all participants' contributions
- **Focus**: Stay on topic and maintain session goals
- **Documentation**: Help capture important decisions and actions
- **Follow-up**: Complete agreed-upon tasks and commitments

## Prohibited Behavior

### Category 1: Safety Violations (Immediate Ban)
**Severity**: Critical
**Response Time**: 24 hours
**Appeal**: 7 days

- Promoting unsafe construction practices
- Ignoring building codes or safety standards
- Providing dangerous technical advice
- Encouraging non-compliance with regulations

### Category 2: Harassment and Abuse (Temporary Ban)
**Severity**: High
**Response Time**: 48 hours
**Appeal**: 14 days

- Personal attacks or bullying
- Targeted harassment or intimidation
- Discriminatory language or behavior
- Stalking or unwanted contact
- Threats of violence or harm

### Category 3: Spam and Misinformation (Warning)
**Severity**: Medium
**Response Time**: 72 hours
**Appeal**: 30 days

- Irrelevant promotional content
- Deliberately false information
- Repetitive or low-quality posts
- Misleading technical claims
- Excessive self-promotion

### Category 4: Professional Misconduct (Review)
**Severity**: Medium
**Response Time**: 5 days
**Appeal**: 30 days

- Plagiarism or copyright violation
- Impersonating professionals
- Unprofessional conduct
- Violating confidentiality agreements
- Conflicts of interest

### Category 5: Minor Violations (Warning)
**Severity**: Low
**Response Time**: 7 days
**Appeal**: 30 days

- Off-topic discussions
- Inappropriate language
- Excessive criticism without solutions
- Poor formatting or presentation
- Minor rule violations

## Content Guidelines

### Technical Content

#### Building Information Modeling (BIM)
- Ensure accuracy in BIM standards and practices
- Reference relevant industry standards (ISO 19650, etc.)
- Provide clear implementation guidance
- Include safety considerations in BIM workflows

#### Construction Technology
- Verify technical specifications and requirements
- Include safety protocols and risk assessments
- Reference applicable codes and standards
- Provide clear documentation and instructions

#### Funding Proposals
- Ensure realistic and achievable project goals
- Include detailed budgets and timelines
- Provide clear deliverables and milestones
- Demonstrate technical feasibility and safety

### Communication Standards

#### Professional Language
- Use clear, professional terminology
- Avoid jargon without explanation
- Maintain respectful tone in all interactions
- Provide context for technical discussions

#### Constructive Feedback
- Focus on content, not individuals
- Provide specific, actionable suggestions
- Acknowledge positive aspects
- Offer solutions, not just criticism

#### Dispute Resolution
- Address issues directly and professionally
- Escalate to moderation when necessary
- Focus on facts and evidence
- Seek common ground and solutions

## Moderation Process

### Moderation Tiers

#### Tier 1: Community Moderators
- **Scope**: Minor violations, content review
- **Actions**: Warnings, content removal, temporary restrictions
- **Response Time**: 72 hours
- **Appeal Process**: 30 days

#### Tier 2: Senior Moderators
- **Scope**: Serious violations, user bans, complex cases
- **Actions**: Temporary bans, escalated warnings, user restrictions
- **Response Time**: 48 hours
- **Appeal Process**: 14 days

#### Tier 3: Community Managers
- **Scope**: Critical violations, permanent bans, policy decisions
- **Actions**: Permanent bans, policy changes, system-wide decisions
- **Response Time**: 24 hours
- **Appeal Process**: 7 days

### Moderation Actions

#### Warnings
- **First Warning**: Educational, no restrictions
- **Second Warning**: 7-day comment restriction
- **Third Warning**: 30-day platform restriction
- **Fourth Warning**: Permanent ban consideration

#### Temporary Restrictions
- **Comment Restrictions**: Limited to 1 comment per day
- **Submission Restrictions**: Cannot submit new drafts
- **Collaboration Restrictions**: Cannot join sessions
- **Funding Restrictions**: Cannot contribute to projects

#### Bans
- **Temporary Ban**: 30-90 days based on severity
- **Permanent Ban**: For repeated or serious violations
- **Appeal Process**: Available for all ban types

### Response Time Standards

| Violation Category | Target Response | Escalation Time |
|-------------------|----------------|-----------------|
| Safety Violations | 24 hours | 12 hours |
| Harassment/Abuse | 48 hours | 24 hours |
| Spam/Misinformation | 72 hours | 48 hours |
| Professional Misconduct | 5 days | 72 hours |
| Minor Violations | 7 days | 5 days |

## Appeals Process

### Appeal Rights
- All moderation actions are appealable
- Appeals must be submitted within the specified timeframe
- Users can provide additional context or evidence
- Appeals are reviewed by senior moderators

### Appeal Process
1. **Submit Appeal**: Use the appeal form with case details
2. **Review Period**: 7-30 days depending on action type
3. **Decision**: Final decision by senior moderator
4. **Implementation**: Action taken based on appeal outcome

### Appeal Grounds
- **New Evidence**: Previously unavailable information
- **Context Missing**: Important context not considered
- **Procedural Error**: Incorrect process followed
- **Disproportionate Action**: Punishment doesn't fit violation
- **Policy Misinterpretation**: Guidelines applied incorrectly

## Reporting Guidelines

### When to Report
- **Safety Concerns**: Unsafe practices or advice
- **Harassment**: Personal attacks or bullying
- **Spam**: Irrelevant or promotional content
- **Misinformation**: False or misleading information
- **Professional Misconduct**: Plagiarism or impersonation
- **Minor Violations**: Rule violations that need attention

### How to Report
1. **Use Report Button**: Available on all content
2. **Select Category**: Choose appropriate violation type
3. **Provide Details**: Explain the specific issue
4. **Include Context**: Relevant background information
5. **Submit Report**: Moderators will review within timeframe

### Report Categories

#### Safety Violations
- Unsafe construction practices
- Code violations
- Dangerous technical advice
- Non-compliance with regulations

#### Harassment and Abuse
- Personal attacks
- Bullying or intimidation
- Discriminatory language
- Threats or stalking

#### Spam and Misinformation
- Irrelevant content
- False information
- Excessive promotion
- Misleading claims

#### Professional Misconduct
- Plagiarism
- Impersonation
- Copyright violation
- Confidentiality breaches

#### Minor Violations
- Off-topic discussions
- Poor formatting
- Inappropriate language
- Rule violations

### Report Response
- **Acknowledgement**: Automatic confirmation within 24 hours
- **Review Timeline**: Response within specified timeframe
- **Status Updates**: Progress notifications for complex cases
- **Resolution**: Final outcome and any actions taken
